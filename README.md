# Refugee Families AI Navigator

## Problem
Refugee and displaced families in the EU face complex systems of legal status, housing, healthcare, education, and employment.
Information is fragmented, difficult to understand, and often unavailable in the user's language.

## Solution
Refugee Families AI Navigator is a humanitarian AI assistant that provides clear, structured, and non-binding information
to help refugee families understand their rights, available services, and next steps in EU countries.

## What is built now (MVP)
- Country-based information structure (YAML)
- Initial countries: Poland, Germany
- Humanitarian and ethical focus
- Documentation-first MVP

## Ethics & Safeguards
- Informational, non-binding guidance only
- No legal or medical advice
- Focus on safety, clarity, and trust
- GDPR-aware, no personal data storage

## Roadmap
- Add more EU countries
- Expand topic depth
- Connect to chatbot (Telegram / Web)
- Partner with NGOs and support organizations
